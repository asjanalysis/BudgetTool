Budget Tool

Upload a budget spreadsheet, attach invoices and proofs, and generate a combined PDF report.

How to use this tool
  1. Choose Template Version (click Version 1 or Version 2 card)
  2. Upload budget spreadsheet OR upload Save Point
  3. Click “Load expenses”
  4. Attach invoices and proofs of payment to lines
  5. Optionally download a Save Point to resume later
  6. Generate final report PDF

Files are read only in your browser—nothing is uploaded or saved on our servers.
